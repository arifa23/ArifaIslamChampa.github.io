Bio

I am a lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at Bangladesh Army International University of Science and Technology (BAIUST). I have completed my B.Sc. in CSE from Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology (RUET). I have got strong in Python, C, C++, Java, MatLab, etc., during my academic years at RUET. Besides my academic pursuits, I actively participated in various co-curricular activities, including online and offline contests such as the National Girls Programming

Contest (NGPC), ACM-ICPC Dhaka Regional, and RUET Day Programming Contest. Furthermore, the projects and undergraduate thesis have greatly helped me put my talents to use in real-world scenarios and boost my communication abilities. During my undergraduate program, I organized and volunteered in several programs, including the RCF Job Fair, the National Collegiate Programming Contest (NCPC), the Science Fair, and the National High School Programming Contest (NHSPC), and some others.


My areas of research interest are artificial intelligence, computer vision, machine learning, data mining, remote sensing, natural language processing, deep learning, and human-computer interaction. I am earnestly seeking a Research Assistantship position where I will prove my devotion and brilliance to accomplish something noteworthy.
